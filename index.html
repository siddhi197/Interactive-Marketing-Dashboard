AI-powered insights & recommendations

Data-backed actions for management to improve ROI and conversion

1 · Double down on Campaign 6's approach
Campaign 6 achieved 14.9% conversion — more than double any predecessor. Analyze what differentiated its creative, timing, and targeting. Replicate and A/B test those variables in future campaigns. Priority: High
2 · Concentrate budget on High Spenders + High Engagement
High Spenders (246 customers) convert at 41.9% with $1,799 avg spend. High Engagement converts at 21.3% with $1,135 avg spend. Together they represent the majority of revenue despite being a minority of customers. Increase marketing budget allocation to these segments. Priority: High
3 · Target no-kids households for campaigns
638 customers without children convert at 26.5% vs 10.3% for parents. Childless households have higher discretionary spending and campaign responsiveness. Segment your campaigns accordingly and personalize messaging. Priority: High
4 · Fix the funnel's first gate — re-engage the 79% who accepted nothing
1,777 customers have never accepted a campaign. Deploy a low-friction re-engagement campaign (e.g., free gift with purchase, loyalty reward) specifically for this dormant segment. Even a 5% reactivation would add ~89 conversions. Priority: Medium
5 · Lead with wines — it's 50% of all revenue
Wine ($681K) and meat products ($374K) account for 78% of total spend. Campaign creative and product promotions should lead with these categories. Consider wine subscription or loyalty bundles. Priority: High
6 · Grow the web channel — highest scalability
Web (27.5%) lags store (39%) but has the lowest marginal cost to scale. Invest in UX improvements, web-exclusive offers, and digital retargeting. Web visitors also represent an ideal remarketing pool for Campaign 7. Priority: Medium
7 · Deprioritize or redesign Campaign 2
At 1.3% acceptance (30 out of 2,240 customers), Campaign 2 severely underperformed every benchmark. Conduct a post-mortem: was it poor timing, wrong audience, weak offer, or channel mismatch? Do not repeat this format. Priority: Critical
Quick summary for stakeholder presentation
The business has a healthy customer base of 2,240 but significant untapped potential — 79% of customers have never responded to any campaign. The highest ROI opportunity is concentrating resources on High Spenders and High Engagement customers who convert at 42% and 21% respectively. Campaign 6's success formula should be studied and replicated. Wines and meat products drive 78% of revenue and must anchor all product promotions. The web channel represents the most scalable growth path. Eliminating spend on Campaign 2-style approaches and redirecting to proven formats would materially improve overall ROI.
